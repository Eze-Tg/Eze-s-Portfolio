Who I Am

2+ Years Coding

I'm a passionate software developer based in Abuja, Nigeria, with a deep love for turning complex problems into elegant, efficient solutions. My journey into development started with a single curiosity — how do websites actually work? — and spiraled into an obsession I haven't looked back from since.

I'm currently focused on mastering the full-stack spectrum — from crafting pixel-perfect UIs with semantic HTML and modern CSS, to building robust backends with Node.js and databases. I believe in writing clean, readable code that other developers (and future me) will thank me for.

Outside of code, you'll find me exploring open-source communities, contributing to developer forums, playing video and board games or experimenting with new tech stacks. I'm driven by the belief that software, done well, genuinely improves people's lives — and I want to be part of building that.
